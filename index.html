Introduction:

The EOD platform aims to facilitate related sustainable design networking and engagements of various stakeholders - end-users, developers, building designers, local authorities, and building managers - from the building design stage to the building occupancy stage.

Building end-users can provide general feedback on their needs to the building's designers.

End-users must be occupants of buildings registered by a developer on the EOD platform in order to provide specific feedback.

Potential buyers can click on the 'Browse New Developments' button to peruse apartment adverts by developers and provide feedback.
